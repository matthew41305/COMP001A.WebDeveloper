Parts

Engine: It is powered by the LS1 V8 engine, a 5.7-liter (346 cubic inches) engine producing approximately 345 horsepower in its initial years and increasing to 350 horsepower in the later models. The engine is known for its reliability and impressive performance.

Transmission: Available with either a 6-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission.

Performance: The C5 Corvette is known for its strong performance and handling. It boasts a 0-60 mph time of around 5.0 seconds and a top speed of about 175 mph.

Content

The C5 Corvette is well-regarded for its balance of performance, technology, and value. It marked a significant improvement over previous generations and set the stage for future models in the Corvette lineup.

The C5 Corvette remains a popular choice among enthusiasts and collectors for its blend of performance, style, and affordability.

Conclusion

The C5 corvette is a notable and well known car in the automotive industry. Despite it's age, the performance to price ratio is still hard to match in the year 2024.

With many options to choose from, whether the base model or the premium Z06, both bodies offer lightweight speed with remarkable handing.

With it being the last car in the U.S that was manufactured with pop-up headlights, you become a walking piece of history.
